Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment where veterans can experience the healing power of equine therapy. Through interaction with horses, we aim to help veterans overcome physical, emotional, and psychological challenges, fostering resilience and promoting overall well-being.

Our Services

Our programs are designed to provide a holistic approach to healing, focusing on the mind, body, and spirit. We offer services that cater to the unique needs of each veteran, helping them rediscover their sense of purpose and find peace in the natural world. Through personalized equine therapy sessions, veterans can build trust and confidence, while our farm activities promote teamwork and resilience. Our serene environment offers a sanctuary for reflection and reconnection, allowing veterans to unwind and heal at their own pace. We are committed to supporting veterans on their journey to recovery, providing them with the tools and experiences they need to thrive.

Ride Horses

Experience the healing power of horseback riding, where veterans connect with gentle, trained horses, building confidence, trust, and a sense of freedom in a calm, supportive setting.

Work the Farm

Engage in hands-on farm activities designed to provide a grounding and purposeful experience, fostering teamwork, resilience, and a renewed sense of accomplishment.

Enjoy the Peace

Unwind in the serene natural surroundings, allowing veterans to find peace, reflect, and reconnect in a tranquil environment far from the stresses of daily life.
